Bais City National Science High School

It is a public science high school in Bais City, Negros Oriental, Philippines. It offers education from Grade 7 to Grade 12. It is recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd) as a science high school.

The STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) strand at Bais City National Science High School provides students with a strong foundation in advanced science and mathematics.
